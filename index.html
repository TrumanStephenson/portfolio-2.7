Yellow Cedar Tribute Page

This project was part of a compilation of certificate projects to get certified for freecodecamp.org. I wanted to dedicate it to the Alaskan Yellow Cedar. It is slowly becomming an endagered species and if better precautions aren't take, we could lose the tree in the coming years. So I wanted to commemorate it's existence eternally.

Ecological Books, Landing Page

This is a landing page for some ecological books I have read and enjoyed. I wanted to showcase their descriptions for others to enjoy. The page even includes links to Amazon, in case someone is interested in purchasing one of them. This was also included as part of the certificate requirement.

Wild-Fire Readiness App

This app was intended to assist with people living in fire prone areas. It provided a check list of supplies necessary for at least 3 days without utilities. As well as, utilized a local weather API with JavaScript to connecte local weather information to the users. It was also intended to give out emergency response alerts if they were in an evacuation zone.

Guide to setup a compound bow

This page was a detailed setup-guide for a compound bow. I practice archery regularly and wanted to dedicate a part of this certficate to one of my hobbies. It is a great sport and the community is very inviting. Take a look at the guide!
